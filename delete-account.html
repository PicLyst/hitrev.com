Delete Account

Effective date: March 13, 2026

PicLyst includes account-based features. This page explains how account deletion requests are handled and what deletion may include.

How to Delete Your Account

If the in-app delete option is available, users should initiate account deletion from within the app by following the instructions in the account or settings area.

If you need help deleting your account, contact us at info@piclyst.com.

What Deletion May Include

When your account is deleted, this may include deletion of:

  • account profile information
  • boards and related organizational structure
  • photos, notes, tags, links, and other user-created content associated with the account
  • saved app data associated with the account, subject to technical and legal limitations

What May Be Retained

Certain limited information may be retained as reasonably necessary to comply with law, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, protect security and integrity, process financial or subscription-related obligations, or maintain required business records.

Timing

We aim to process deletion requests within a reasonable period. Some limited information may remain temporarily in backups, logs, or system records before being fully removed.

Subscriptions

Deleting your PicLyst account does not automatically cancel an Apple subscription. Subscriptions managed through Apple must be canceled separately through your Apple account.

Questions

For help with account deletion or other privacy-related requests, contact info@piclyst.com.

You can also review our Privacy Policy and Support page for more information.
