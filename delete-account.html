Account help

Delete your PicLyst account

This page explains how to request or complete deletion of your PicLyst account and associated account data.

How to delete your account

If account deletion is available inside the PicLyst app, you can follow the account deletion option provided in the app settings.

You may also request account deletion by emailing info@piclyst.com.

What to include in your request

Please include the email address associated with your PicLyst account and state clearly that you are requesting account deletion.

We may need to verify your request before deleting the account to help protect your privacy and prevent unauthorized deletion.

What is deleted

Account deletion is intended to remove your PicLyst account and associated app data, including account-related records and content associated with that account where applicable.

What may be retained

Some limited information may be retained if required for legal, security, fraud prevention, accounting, dispute resolution, or other legitimate business purposes.

Contact

For account deletion requests or related questions, email info@piclyst.com.

For general company questions, contact Hit Rev Enterprises, LLC at hitrev@gmail.com.
