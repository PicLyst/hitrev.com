Delete Account

Effective date: March 13, 2026

If PicLyst offers account creation, users may be able to initiate account deletion from within the app. This page explains the general process and what deletion may include.

How to Request Deletion

If the in-app delete option is available, please follow the instructions provided in the app settings or account area.

If you need help with account deletion, contact us at hitrev@gmail.com.

What Deletion May Include

When an account is deleted, this may include deletion of:

  • account profile information
  • saved app content associated with the account
  • photos, notes, tags, links, and related user-created data, subject to technical and legal limitations

What May Be Retained

Certain limited information may be retained as reasonably necessary to comply with law, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, protect security and integrity, or maintain required business records.

Timing

We aim to process deletion requests within a reasonable period, though some information may remain in backups or logs for a limited time before being fully removed.

Questions

For questions about account deletion or privacy-related requests, contact hitrev@gmail.com.

You can also review our Privacy Policy and Support page for more information.
