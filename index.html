Featured product

PicLyst

PicLyst is a visual organization app that helps people keep track of spaces, items, and information by attaching tags, notes, links, checklists, and layered details directly to images.

Visual boards

Start with a photo or image and organize information visually.

Photo-based tags

Add tags to specific areas so details stay connected to the right place.

Notes and checklists

Store reminders, instructions, inventory details, and task lists.

Layered details

Keep deeper information organized without cluttering the main view.

Designed for everyday organization

PicLyst can help with home organization, storage boxes, manuals, appliance information, photo-based notes, and other real-world details that are easier to manage visually.
