Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Hit Rev Enterprises, LLC handles information related to PicLyst.

Information we collect

PicLyst may collect account information, app usage information, and content you choose to create or upload while using the app, such as boards, photos, notes, checklists, links, and related organizational details.

We use this information to provide, maintain, improve, and support the PicLyst app.

How information is used

Information may be used to operate the app, provide account access, sync content where applicable, respond to support requests, maintain security, troubleshoot problems, and improve the user experience.

Content you add to PicLyst

You are responsible for the photos, notes, links, checklists, and other content you add to PicLyst. You should avoid adding sensitive information unless you are comfortable storing it in the app.

Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only when needed to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, protect rights and security, or use trusted service providers that help operate the app.

Account deletion

You may request or complete account deletion as described on the Delete Account page.

For PicLyst support, email info@piclyst.com.

Changes to this policy
