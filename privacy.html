Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026

Hit Rev Enterprises, LLC (“Hit Rev,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) provides PicLyst. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and manage information in connection with the PicLyst app, this website, and related support services.

PicLyst is a visual organization app that helps users organize information by attaching notes, links, and related details to images.

Information We Collect

Depending on how PicLyst is used, we may collect or process:

  • account information, such as email address, login details, or account identifiers
  • user content, such as photos, notes, tags, links, boards, and other organizational data created in the app
  • subscription or purchase-related status needed to manage access to paid features
  • support communications you send to us
  • basic technical information needed to operate, maintain, and secure the app
  • diagnostic or performance information if such tools are enabled

How We Use Information

We may use information to:

  • provide, operate, and maintain PicLyst
  • create and manage user accounts
  • save, sync, and display user content across supported experiences
  • provide access to free or paid features
  • respond to support requests and user inquiries
  • improve app functionality, reliability, and security
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

User Content and Cloud Storage

PicLyst stores and syncs user-created content, including photos, notes, tags, links, boards, and related visual organization data, in order to provide the app’s features.

Subscriptions and Purchases

If PicLyst offers paid features or subscriptions, purchase transactions may be processed by Apple. We may receive limited information such as subscription status or entitlement information necessary to provide access to those features.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only as reasonably necessary to:

  • operate the app and related services
  • work with service providers that help support hosting, storage, authentication, or app functionality
  • comply with law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request
  • protect the rights, safety, security, and integrity of users, our services, or others

Data Retention

We may retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide PicLyst, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain legitimate business records.

Account Deletion

PicLyst allows users to initiate account deletion within the app. When an account is deleted, associated account data, boards, and stored user content for that account are removed, subject to limited retention as described in this policy. Additional information is available on our Delete Account page.

Data Security

We use reasonable measures intended to protect information, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

PicLyst is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or privacy-related requests, contact us at info@piclyst.com.
